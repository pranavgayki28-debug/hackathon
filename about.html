About COSA

COSA (Computer Engineering Studentsâ€™ Association) is a student-driven professional body formed with the vision of empowering Computer Engineering students through technical excellence, innovation, and collaboration. COSA acts as a platform where students can enhance their technical skills, leadership qualities, and industry readiness beyond the academic curriculum.

The association organizes a wide range of activities including technical workshops, seminars, coding events, project exhibitions, industrial talks, hackathons, and expert sessions to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. COSA encourages students to explore emerging technologies, develop problem-solving skills, and stay updated with current industry trends.

At COSA, we bring together engineers, innovators, and tech enthusiasts from across the nation. The festival features lectures, workshops, exhibitions, and exciting competitions covering the latest trends in technology.

COSA also focuses on overall personality development by promoting teamwork, communication skills, and leadership through events, competitions, and collaborative projects. By fostering a culture of learning, creativity, and innovation, COSA aims to prepare students to become competent professionals and responsible contributors to the technology-driven world
