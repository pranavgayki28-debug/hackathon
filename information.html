About

The Problem Analysis Competition 2026 is a technical event organized for students to showcase their database management skills, logical thinking, and query optimization abilities. The tournament focuses on promoting technical excellence and analytical skills through SQL challenges.

Prize Pool

Competition Rounds

01

MCQ Rounds

The objective of the MCQ round is to evaluate participants’ conceptual clarity in SQL and identify eligible candidates for advanced rounds by measuring their accuracy, basic problem-solving ability, and time management skills.

Time Limit: 10 minutes

02

Query Writing

The objective of the Query Writing round is to assess hands-on SQL skills, logical thinking, and the ability to translate given requirements into accurate executable SQL queries within a limited time.

Time Limit: 20 minutes

03

Debug Query

The goal of the Debug Query round is to test participants’ ability to identify, analyze, and correct errors in SQL queries, demonstrating deep understanding of SQL syntax, logic, and query execution.

Time Limit: 10 minutes
